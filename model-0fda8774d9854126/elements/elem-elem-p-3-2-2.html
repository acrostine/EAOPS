Community engagement and dialogue ()
A scheduled engagement session is initiated by an EA practitioner, resulting in a standards update proposal submitted to architecture principles definition. The process encompasses preparing the session agenda, running the engagement session, handling questions and challenges, compiling the engagement summary, and routing outputs to governance and feedback channels. Key stakeholders include the EA practitioner, stakeholders, and the architecture lead, all of whom contribute to the effective communication and collaboration necessary for successful community engagement and dialogue. The process exists to ensure that community members and stakeholders are actively involved in the architecture development lifecycle, facilitating valuable feedback and insights that enhance project outcomes. Without this structured engagement, the quality of stakeholder input may degrade, leading to misalignment between community needs and architectural decisions. The primary output of this process is the standards update proposal, which is submitted to the architecture principles definition. <!-- SPEC --> **Lifecycle phases** - prepare session agenda: outline objectives and topics for discussion - run engagement session: facilitate dialogue and gather input from participants - handle questions and challenges: address concerns and clarify points raised by stakeholders - compile engagement summary: document key insights and feedback from the session - route outputs to governance and feedback channels: ensure findings are communicated to relevant decision-makers **Information objects** - community update: produced by community engagement, arrives at run engagement session, passed to compile engagement summary - architecture adoption metrics: produced by architecture assessment, arrives at run engagement session, passed to compile engagement summary - stakeholder view: produced by stakeholder input, arrives at run engagement session, passed to compile engagement summary - architecture model: produced by architecture development, arrives at route outputs to governance and feedback channels, passed to standards update proposal - standards update proposal: produced by compile engagement summary, arrives at route outputs to governance and feedback channels, passed to architecture principles definition
