Delivery team coaching ()
The delivery team coaching process is initiated when selected architecture patterns and an impact analysis report are received, or when project support feedback is submitted by stakeholders. This process encompasses the planning and execution of coaching engagements aimed at enhancing the project team's alignment with architectural standards, involving decisions made by EA practitioners and architecture leads throughout its lifecycle. It exists to ensure that project teams receive the necessary guidance and support to effectively implement architectural principles, thereby preventing misalignments and inefficiencies; the primary output is coaching outcomes and architecture guidance delivered to the project team. <!-- SPEC --> **Lifecycle phases** - receive patterns and impact findings: gather selected architecture patterns and impact analysis reports to inform the coaching process - plan coaching engagement: outline the objectives, schedule, and resources for the coaching sessions - deliver coaching sessions: conduct sessions to provide guidance and support to the project team - address questions and deviations: respond to inquiries and manage any deviations from the planned coaching approach - close engagement and capture outcomes: finalize the coaching process and document the outcomes for future reference **Information objects** - architecture model: produced by upstream source, arrives at phase receive patterns and impact findings, passed to downstream phase plan coaching engagement - impact assessment: produced by upstream source, arrives at phase receive patterns and impact findings, passed to downstream phase plan coaching engagement - architecture decision: produced by upstream source, arrives at phase close engagement and capture outcomes, passed to downstream phase not applicable - architecture principle: produced by upstream source, arrives at phase close engagement and capture outcomes, passed to downstream phase not applicable - stakeholder feedback: produced by upstream source, arrives at phase receive patterns and impact findings, passed to downstream phase plan coaching engagement
