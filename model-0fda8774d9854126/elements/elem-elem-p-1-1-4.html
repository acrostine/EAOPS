Information transfer and repository seeding ()
The process is initiated when a mapping document is received from the source-to-meta-model mapping, confirming readiness to import. This process encompasses the preparation and execution of information transfer and repository seeding, which involves several key stakeholders including the EA practitioner, architecture lead, and the repository system. Throughout its lifecycle, the process ensures that the foundational architecture repository is established and aligned with strategic objectives, making critical decisions at various stages to validate the integrity of the data being imported. The purpose of this process is to facilitate the seamless transfer of information into the architecture repository, thereby ensuring that the repository is populated with accurate and relevant data. Without this process, the repository may lack the necessary information to support strategic decision-making and compliance with architectural standards. The primary output of this process is a populated repository, which is delivered to the EA strategic direction and roadmap and architecture principles definition. <!-- SPEC --> **Lifecycle phases** - receive mapping document: the process begins with the receipt of the mapping document confirming readiness for import. - prepare source artefacts: relevant source artefacts are identified and prepared for import into the repository. - execute import: the prepared artefacts are imported into the architecture repository. - validate populated repository: the imported data is validated to ensure accuracy and completeness. - publish and hand off: the populated repository is finalized and handed off to the relevant stakeholders. **Information objects** - seeding artefact: produced by upstream source, arrives at prepare source artefacts, passed to execute import - meta-model specification: produced by upstream source, arrives at execute import, passed to validate populated repository - mapping document: produced by upstream source, arrives at receive mapping document, passed to prepare source artefacts - architecture model: produced by upstream source, arrives at validate populated repository, passed to publish and hand off - populated repository: produced by execute import, arrives at validate populated repository, passed to publish and hand off
