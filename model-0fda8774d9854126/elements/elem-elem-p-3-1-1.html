Stakeholder view design ()
The scheduled publication cycle begins or a stakeholder requests a new audience-specific view, initiating the stakeholder view design process. This process focuses on creating tailored views of architectural information that meet the needs of different stakeholders, progressing through several defined phases from identifying target audiences to publishing the designed views. It involves key decisions made by EA practitioners and architecture leads, ensuring that the final outputs align with the overarching architecture strategy and meet stakeholder expectations. The stakeholder view design process exists to enhance the communication of architectural information, ensuring that it is relevant and accessible to various audiences. Without this process, the dissemination of architectural insights would be inconsistent and potentially misaligned with stakeholder needs, leading to confusion and ineffective decision-making. The primary output of this process is the designed stakeholder views, which are delivered to the model-once, view-many execution phase. <!-- SPEC --> **Lifecycle phases** - identify target audiences: determine who the views are intended for - define view scope and filters: establish the content and parameters for the views - design view layout and notation: create the visual representation of the views - review against architecture strategy: ensure alignment with the overall architecture strategy - publish designed views: finalize and distribute the views to stakeholders **Information objects** - stakeholder view: produced by stakeholder view design, arrives at publish designed views, passed to model-once, view-many execution - architecture model: produced by architecture publishing, arrives at define view scope and filters, passed to design view layout and notation - modelling conventions document: produced by architecture publishing, arrives at design view layout and notation, passed to review against architecture strategy - architecture strategy review: produced by architecture strategy, arrives at review against architecture strategy, passed to publish designed views - architecture decision: produced by architecture governance, arrives at review against architecture strategy, passed to publish designed views
