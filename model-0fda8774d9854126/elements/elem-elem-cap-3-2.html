Community & Stakeholder Engagement ()
Community & Stakeholder Engagement executes stakeholder engagement at the Execute level, ensuring effective communication and collaboration with community members and stakeholders. It progresses through architecture content creation, community engagement and dialogue, stakeholder feedback collection, and architecture adoption measurement, from initial engagement to feedback integration. It produces Knowledge Base Update Request for Repository & Knowledge Management, enabling the enhancement of shared knowledge resources. It also produces Project Support Feedback for Architecture Delivery & Project Support, enabling improved project outcomes.
