Repository & Knowledge Management ()
"Repository & Knowledge Management executes control over knowledge assets at the Execute level, ensuring effective management and utilization of architectural knowledge. It progresses through model creation and curation, modelling conventions management, repository evolution planning, and repository quality and hygiene, from initiation to continuous improvement. It receives Published Architecture Feedback from Architecture Publishing and Knowledge Base Update Request from Community & Stakeholder Engagement to enhance repository content and quality. It produces no outbound flows, focusing solely on internal management and improvement of knowledge assets."
