Project intake and architecture scoping ()
A project request is submitted by a delivery team requiring an engagement assessment from the Enterprise Architecture (EA) team. The process of project intake and architecture scoping involves assessing the relevance of the project to architectural standards, defining the scope and engagement level, identifying applicable architecture patterns, and issuing the necessary outputs for further analysis and pattern provision. Stakeholders such as the project team, architecture lead, and EA practitioner collaborate throughout this lifecycle to ensure that the project aligns with established architectural principles and frameworks. The process exists to ensure that projects are effectively aligned with architectural standards, providing clarity and direction for the delivery teams. Without this structured approach, projects may deviate from architectural guidelines, leading to inefficiencies and potential misalignment with organizational goals. The primary output of this process is the architecture scope and initial impact information, which is delivered to impact analysis and scoped architecture patterns to pattern provision. <!-- SPEC --> **Lifecycle phases** - receive project request: the delivery team submits a request for EA engagement assessment - assess architecture relevance: the EA team evaluates the project's alignment with architectural standards - define scope and engagement level: the scope of the project and the level of engagement required are determined - identify applicable patterns: relevant architecture patterns are identified for the project - issue scope outputs to analysis and pattern provision: outputs are delivered to impact analysis and scoped architecture patterns **Information objects** - impact assessment: produced by upstream source, arrives at phase assess architecture relevance, passed to downstream phase issue scope outputs to analysis and pattern provision - architecture model: produced by upstream source, arrives at phase identify applicable patterns, passed to downstream phase issue scope outputs to analysis and pattern provision - architecture principle: produced by upstream source, arrives at phase define scope and engagement level, passed to downstream phase issue scope outputs to analysis and pattern provision - architecture decision: produced by upstream source, arrives at phase define scope and engagement level, passed to downstream phase issue scope outputs to analysis and pattern provision - roadmap: produced by upstream source, arrives at phase issue scope outputs to analysis and pattern provision, passed to downstream phase issue scope outputs to analysis and pattern provision
