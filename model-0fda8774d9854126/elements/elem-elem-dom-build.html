Build ()
Build governs Repository & Knowledge Management and Repository Initialisation & Strategy across the enterprise architecture domain, ensuring effective management of architectural knowledge and resources. It produces Architecture outputs and leadership conversation output for Govern and receives Published views and feedback, Published Architecture Feedback, and structured strategic input from Deliver and Govern to inform architectural decisions. It does not cover implementation or operational activities — that accountability rests with the Delivery domain.
