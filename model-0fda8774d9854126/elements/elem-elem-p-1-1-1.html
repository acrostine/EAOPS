Current state assessment ()
An EA initiation event or a strategic review request raised by leadership triggers the Current state assessment process, resulting in the creation of a current state report. This process involves defining the assessment scope, gathering evidence, analyzing gaps, drafting the current state report, and validating and publishing the findings. Key stakeholders, including the EA practitioner, architecture lead, and other stakeholders, engage throughout the lifecycle to ensure that the assessment aligns with the overall strategic direction and repository initialization. The Current state assessment exists to provide a clear understanding of the existing architectural landscape, which is essential for informed decision-making and strategic planning. Without this process, organizations may struggle to identify gaps and misalignments in their architecture, leading to inefficiencies and potential non-compliance with architectural standards. The primary output, the current state report, is distributed to the meta-model design and strategic intent discovery teams to facilitate further development and alignment. <!-- SPEC --> **Lifecycle phases** - define assessment scope: identify the boundaries and objectives of the assessment - gather evidence: collect relevant data and information from various sources - analyse gaps: evaluate the current state against desired outcomes and identify discrepancies - draft current state report: compile findings and insights into a structured document - validate and publish: review the report for accuracy and disseminate it to stakeholders **Information objects** - current state report: produced by the Current state assessment, arrives at meta-model design, passed to strategic intent discovery - architecture model: produced by the Current state assessment, arrives at meta-model design, passed to strategic intent discovery - roadmap: produced by the Current state assessment, arrives at meta-model design, passed to strategic intent discovery - leadership conversation output: produced by the Current state assessment, arrives at Strategic Alignment & Leadership Engagement, passed to architecture lead
