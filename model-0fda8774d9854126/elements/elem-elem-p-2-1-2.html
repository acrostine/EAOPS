Solution design review ()
The solution design review process is initiated when a project team submits a solution design for architecture review. This process results in the delivery of solution design review feedback to the deviation and exception management team. The process encompasses several key activities, including receiving the review request, applying architecture principles, identifying conformance gaps, formulating review feedback, and issuing findings to deviation management. Stakeholders involved in this process include the architecture lead, EA practitioner, and the project team, all of whom play critical roles in ensuring that the solution aligns with established architectural standards throughout its lifecycle. This process exists to ensure that all solution designs adhere to the defined architecture principles, thereby maintaining compliance and mitigating risks associated with deviations. Without this review, the organization may face challenges in upholding architectural integrity, leading to potential inefficiencies and inconsistencies in project outcomes. The primary output of this process is the solution design review feedback, which is delivered to the deviation and exception management team. <!-- SPEC --> **Lifecycle phases** - receive review request: the project team submits a solution design for review. - apply architecture principles: the architecture principles are applied to the submitted design. - identify conformance gaps: gaps between the design and architecture principles are identified. - formulate review feedback: feedback is formulated based on the identified gaps. - issue finding to deviation management: the findings are issued to the deviation and exception management team. **Information objects** - architecture principle: produced by upstream source, arrives at apply architecture principles, passed to identify conformance gaps. - compliance report: produced by upstream source, arrives at issue finding to deviation management, passed to architecture decision. - deviation record: produced by upstream source, arrives at issue finding to deviation management, passed to impact assessment. - architecture decision: produced by upstream source, arrives at issue finding to deviation management, passed to architecture compliance report. - impact assessment: produced by upstream source, arrives at issue finding to deviation management, passed to architecture compliance report.
