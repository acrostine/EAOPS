Architecture Standards & Compliance ()
Architecture Standards & Compliance controls architecture standards at the Control level, ensuring compliance with established architectural principles. It progresses through architecture principles definition, compliance monitoring and reporting, deviation and exception management, and solution design review, from initial definition to final reporting. It receives Standards Update Proposal from Community & Stakeholder Engagement to ensure alignment with stakeholder needs. It produces Architecture Compliance Report for Architecture Publishing, enabling informed decision-making regarding architectural adherence.
