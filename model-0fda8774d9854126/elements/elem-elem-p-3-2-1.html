Architecture content creation ()
Stakeholder feedback is received from stakeholder feedback collection or a scheduled content cycle begins, resulting in the initiation of the architecture content creation process. This process encompasses several phases, including reviewing stakeholder feedback, identifying content needs, drafting content, reviewing and editing, and ultimately publishing the updated architecture content to the engagement channel. Key stakeholders involved in this process include EA practitioners, architecture leads, and various stakeholders who contribute to and benefit from the architecture content. The architecture content creation process exists to ensure that communication and collaboration with community members and stakeholders are effective and meaningful. By systematically engaging with stakeholders and integrating their feedback, the process enhances the quality of architecture content and fosters a shared understanding of architectural decisions. Without this structured approach, the risk of misalignment between stakeholder expectations and architectural outcomes increases, leading to potential project failures. The primary output of this process is the updated architecture content, which is delivered to community engagement and dialogue. <!-- SPEC --> **Lifecycle phases** - review stakeholder feedback: assess and analyze the feedback received from stakeholders - identify content need: determine what content is required based on feedback and engagement - draft content: create initial drafts of the architecture content - review and edit: refine the drafted content through reviews and edits - publish to engagement channel: disseminate the finalized content to the appropriate engagement channels **Information objects** - community update: produced by stakeholder feedback, arrives at identify content need, passed to draft content - stakeholder view: produced by stakeholder feedback, arrives at review stakeholder feedback, passed to identify content need - architecture model: produced by architecture decision, arrives at draft content, passed to publish to engagement channel - stakeholder feedback: produced by stakeholder feedback collection, arrives at review stakeholder feedback, passed to identify content need - architecture decision: produced by architecture model, arrives at draft content, passed to publish to engagement channel
