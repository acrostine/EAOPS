Review and approval coordination ()
The process is initiated when validated model changes are received from model development and semantic validation, along with cycle management feedback from stakeholder feedback collection. The Review and Approval Coordination process encompasses the entire lifecycle of reviewing and approving model changes, involving key stakeholders such as the architecture lead, review board, and stakeholders. Throughout its phases, the process ensures that all feedback is collected, consolidated, and assessed to reach a final decision on the approval of model changes, ultimately leading to the issuance of approved model changes. The Review and Approval Coordination process exists to provide structured governance over the release cycle, ensuring that all changes are thoroughly vetted and aligned with architectural standards. Without this process, the quality and compliance of model changes could degrade, leading to potential misalignment and inefficiencies in the release cycle. The primary output of this process is the approved model changes, which are delivered to the release authorisation and publication phase. <!-- SPEC --> **Lifecycle phases** - receive validated model changes: the process begins with the reception of validated model changes and cycle management feedback. - assign reviewers and set schedule: reviewers are assigned, and a schedule for the review process is established. - collect review inputs: inputs from the assigned reviewers are collected to gather diverse perspectives on the model changes. - consolidate feedback and reach disposition: feedback is consolidated, and a disposition is reached regarding the approval of the model changes. - issue approved model changes: the final approved model changes are issued for release authorisation and publication. **Information objects** - architecture model: produced by upstream source, arrives at receive validated model changes, passed to consolidate feedback and reach disposition. - architecture decision: produced by upstream source, arrives at consolidate feedback and reach disposition, passed to issue approved model changes. - compliance report: produced by upstream source, arrives at consolidate feedback and reach disposition, passed to issue approved model changes. - release package: produced by upstream source, arrives at issue approved model changes, passed to release authorisation and publication. - approved model changes: produced by upstream source, arrives at issue approved model changes, passed to release authorisation and publication.
