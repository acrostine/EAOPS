Architecture Delivery & Project Support ()
Architecture Delivery & Project Support executes control objects at the Execute level, ensuring effective project alignment with architectural standards. It progresses through the phases of architecture pattern provision, delivery team coaching, impact analysis, and project intake and architecture scoping, from initiation to completion. It receives Project Support Feedback from Community & Stakeholder Engagement to inform project adjustments and improvements. It produces no outbound information objects for other capabilities, enabling a focused internal support mechanism.
