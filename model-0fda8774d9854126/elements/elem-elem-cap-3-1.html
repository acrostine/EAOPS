Architecture Publishing ()
Architecture Publishing executes control objects at the Execute level, ensuring the effective dissemination of architectural information. It progresses through phases of HTML publication management, model-once, view-many execution, publication quality assurance, and stakeholder view design, from initiation to final publication. It receives Architecture Compliance Reports and Release Approval Documentation from Architecture Standards & Compliance and Release Cycle Management to ensure adherence to standards and approval processes. It produces Architecture Strategy Reviews and Published Architecture Feedback for Repository Initialisation & Strategy and Repository & Knowledge Management, enabling informed decision-making and knowledge sharing.
