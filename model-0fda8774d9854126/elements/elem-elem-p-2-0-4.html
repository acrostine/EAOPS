Vision stewardship ()
The scheduled vision review cycle or a significant strategic event requiring architectural vision reassessment initiates the vision stewardship process, resulting in a revised architectural vision published as a living reference asset. This process encompasses the assessment of the current vision's relevance against the organization's strategy, updating vision statements as necessary, and validating these updates with leadership to ensure alignment. The vision stewardship process exists to maintain the coherence of the architectural vision with the strategic goals of the organization, delivering value by ensuring that the vision remains relevant and actionable; without it, misalignment could lead to ineffective strategy execution. The primary output is the revised architectural vision, which is received by Repository Initialisation & Strategy. <!-- SPEC --> **Lifecycle phases** - schedule vision review: initiate the review process based on a predetermined schedule or significant event - assess vision currency against strategy: evaluate the current vision's alignment with the organization's strategic objectives - update vision statements: revise the vision statements to reflect any necessary changes identified during the assessment - validate with leadership: confirm the updated vision statements with leadership to ensure buy-in and alignment - publish revised vision: disseminate the revised architectural vision as a living reference asset **Information objects** - roadmap: produced by upstream source, arrives at schedule vision review, passed to assess vision currency against strategy - architecture principle: produced by upstream source, arrives at assess vision currency against strategy, passed to update vision statements - architecture model: produced by upstream source, arrives at assess vision currency against strategy, passed to update vision statements - architecture decision: produced by upstream source, arrives at validate with leadership, passed to publish revised vision - structured strategic input: produced by upstream source, arrives at publish revised vision, passed to Repository Initialisation & Strategy
