Strategic Alignment & Leadership Engagement ()
Strategic Alignment & Leadership Engagement directs control objects at the Direct level, ensuring effective alignment of strategic initiatives with organizational goals. It progresses through phases of architecture-to-strategy translation, investment coherence and portfolio sequencing, strategic intent discovery, and vision stewardship, from initial alignment to ongoing leadership engagement. It receives leadership conversation output from Repository Initialisation & Strategy to facilitate informed decision-making. It produces structured strategic input for Repository Initialisation & Strategy, enabling coherent strategy execution and initiative sequencing decision for Release Cycle Management.
