Deliver ()
Deliver governs Architecture Publishing and Community & Stakeholder Engagement across the enterprise architecture domain, ensuring effective communication and collaboration with stakeholders. It produces Published views and feedback, Stakeholder and community feedback, Published Architecture Feedback, and Standards Update Proposal for the Build and Govern domains and receives Approved architecture artefacts and Architecture Compliance Report from the Govern domain to ensure alignment with established standards. It does not cover the development and implementation of architecture artefacts — that accountability rests with the Build domain.
