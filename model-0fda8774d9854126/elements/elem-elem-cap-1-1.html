Repository Initialisation & Strategy ()
Repository Initialisation & Strategy executes control objects at the Execute level, ensuring the establishment of a foundational architecture repository and strategic alignment. It progresses through Current state assessment, EA strategic direction and roadmap, Information transfer and repository seeding, and Meta-model design, from assessment to mapping. It receives Architecture Strategy Review from Architecture Publishing and structured strategic input from Strategic Alignment & Leadership Engagement to inform repository initialization and strategy development. It produces Initial Architecture Blueprint for Architecture Standards & Compliance and leadership conversation output for Strategic Alignment & Leadership Engagement, enabling strategic coherence and compliance with architectural standards.
