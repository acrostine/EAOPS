EA strategic direction and roadmap ()
The process is initiated when a populated repository is delivered through information transfer and repository seeding, or when a scheduled review cycle begins, resulting in the creation of an EA strategic roadmap document. This process encompasses the assessment of the current state, the formulation of the EA strategic direction and roadmap, sequencing of capability investments, drafting of the roadmap document, and validation with leadership. Key stakeholders, including the architecture lead, stakeholders, and the review board, engage throughout the lifecycle to ensure that the repository is aligned with strategic objectives and architectural standards. The EA strategic direction and roadmap process exists to deliver value by establishing a coherent architectural strategy that guides multi-year capability investments. Without this structured approach, organizations risk misalignment between their architectural initiatives and strategic goals, leading to inefficiencies and potential compliance issues. The primary output of this process is the EA strategic roadmap document, which is delivered to the Strategic Alignment & Leadership Engagement stakeholders. <!-- SPEC --> **Lifecycle phases** - consolidate inputs: gather and assess existing information and strategic inputs - formulate strategic direction: define the overarching strategic goals and objectives - sequence capability investments: prioritize and schedule investments based on strategic alignment - draft roadmap document: create a comprehensive document outlining the strategic direction and investments - validate with leadership: review and confirm the roadmap with key leadership stakeholders **Information objects** - roadmap: produced by drafting the roadmap document, arrives at validate with leadership, passed to Strategic Alignment & Leadership Engagement - architecture model: produced by consolidating inputs, arrives at formulate strategic direction, passed to sequence capability investments - seeding artefact: produced by information transfer and repository seeding, arrives at consolidate inputs, passed to formulate strategic direction - architecture strategy review: produced by Architecture Publishing, arrives at consolidate inputs, passed to formulate strategic direction - structured strategic input: produced by Strategic Alignment & Leadership Engagement, arrives at consolidate inputs, passed to formulate strategic direction
