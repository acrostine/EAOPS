Stakeholder feedback collection ()
A stakeholder submits feedback or a structured solicitation cycle begins following a publication or engagement session, resulting in the collection of stakeholder feedback. The process of stakeholder feedback collection encompasses soliciting or receiving feedback, classifying it by type, assessing its relevance and urgency, compiling a feedback record, and routing it to consuming processes. Key stakeholders involved include the stakeholder providing feedback, EA practitioners who facilitate the process, and the architecture lead who oversees the integration of feedback into architecture content. The stakeholder feedback collection process exists to ensure that community members and stakeholders have a voice in the architecture development process, ultimately enhancing the quality and relevance of the architecture outputs. Without this process, valuable insights from stakeholders may be overlooked, leading to a disconnect between the architecture and community needs.
