Vision stewardship ()
The scheduled vision review cycle or a significant strategic event requiring architectural vision reassessment initiates the vision stewardship process, resulting in a revised architectural vision published as a living reference asset. This process encompasses the assessment of the current vision's relevance against the organization's strategy, updating vision statements as necessary, and validating these updates with leadership to ensure alignment. The vision stewardship process exists to maintain the coherence of the architectural vision with the strategic goals of the organization, delivering value by ensuring that the vision remains relevant and actionable; without it, misalignment could lead to ineffective strategy execution.
