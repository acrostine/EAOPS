Information transfer and repository seeding ()
The process is initiated when a mapping document is received from the source-to-meta-model mapping, confirming readiness to import. This process encompasses the preparation and execution of information transfer and repository seeding, which involves several key stakeholders including the EA practitioner, architecture lead, and the repository system. Throughout its lifecycle, the process ensures that the foundational architecture repository is established and aligned with strategic objectives, making critical decisions at various stages to validate the integrity of the data being imported. The purpose of this process is to facilitate the seamless transfer of information into the architecture repository, thereby ensuring that the repository is populated with accurate and relevant data. Without this process, the repository may lack the necessary information to support strategic decision-making and compliance with architectural standards.
