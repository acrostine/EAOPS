Architecture pattern provision ()
A scoped architecture patterns request is received from project intake and architecture scoping, initiating the architecture pattern provision process. This process involves several key phases, including receiving the pattern request, searching and selecting candidate patterns, contextualizing those patterns for the specific project, packaging the selected patterns, and finally handing them off to delivery team coaching. Stakeholders such as EA practitioners, architecture leads, and project teams are engaged throughout the lifecycle to ensure alignment with architectural standards and project requirements. The architecture pattern provision process exists to deliver value by ensuring that projects are equipped with the appropriate architectural patterns tailored to their specific needs. Without this process, projects may struggle with misalignment to architectural standards, resulting in inefficiencies and potential project failures.
