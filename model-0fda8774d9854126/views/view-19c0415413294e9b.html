Model creation and curation - Context ()
A modelling request is received from a stakeholder feedback, roadmap directive, or conventions update, resulting in an architecture model that is updated and submitted to the repository quality and hygiene. The process encompasses the intake of requests, application of modelling conventions, creation or updating of models, validation of their structure, and submission for quality review, involving stakeholders such as EA practitioners, architecture leads, and the repository system. This process exists to ensure that architectural knowledge is effectively managed and utilized, enhancing the quality and relevance of the repository. Without this process, the integrity and usability of knowledge assets would degrade, leading to potential misalignment in architectural practices.
