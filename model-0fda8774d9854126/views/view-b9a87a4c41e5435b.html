EA strategic direction and roadmap - Context ()
The process is initiated when a populated repository is delivered through information transfer and repository seeding, or when a scheduled review cycle begins, resulting in the creation of an EA strategic roadmap document. This process encompasses the assessment of the current state, the formulation of the EA strategic direction and roadmap, sequencing of capability investments, drafting of the roadmap document, and validation with leadership. Key stakeholders, including the architecture lead, stakeholders, and the review board, engage throughout the lifecycle to ensure that the repository is aligned with strategic objectives and architectural standards. The EA strategic direction and roadmap process exists to deliver value by establishing a coherent architectural strategy that guides multi-year capability investments. Without this structured approach, organizations risk misalignment between their architectural initiatives and strategic goals, leading to inefficiencies and potential compliance issues.
