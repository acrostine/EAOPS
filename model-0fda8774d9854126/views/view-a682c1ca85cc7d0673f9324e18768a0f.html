Modelling conventions management ()
A scheduled review cycle is initiated by the standards manager or an exception is raised against current conventions, resulting in an updated modelling conventions document. The process encompasses the evaluation and enhancement of existing modelling conventions, involving stakeholders such as the architecture lead, EA practitioners, and the review board. It progresses through defined phases from scheduling a review to publishing the updated conventions, ensuring that architectural knowledge is effectively managed and utilized. The process exists to maintain the integrity and relevance of modelling conventions, which are crucial for consistent architectural practices. Without this process, the quality of architectural knowledge could degrade, leading to inconsistencies and inefficiencies in model creation and curation.
